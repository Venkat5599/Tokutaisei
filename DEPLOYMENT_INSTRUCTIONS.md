# 🚀 Smart Contract Deployment Guide

## Step-by-Step Deployment to Sepolia Testnet

### Prerequisites Checklist
- [ ] MetaMask installed and set up
- [ ] MetaMask switched to Sepolia Test Network
- [ ] Have at least 0.1 Sepolia ETH in your wallet

---

## Step 1: Get Sepolia Test ETH (5 minutes)

### Option A: Alchemy Faucet (Recommended)
1. Go to: https://www.alchemy.com/faucets/ethereum-sepolia
2. Sign in with your account (free)
3. Enter your wallet address
4. Click "Send Me ETH"
5. Wait 1-2 minutes for ETH to arrive

### Option B: Infura Faucet
1. Go to: https://www.infura.io/faucet/sepolia
2. Sign in with your account
3. Enter your wallet address
4. Complete verification
5. Receive 0.5 Sepolia ETH

### Option C: Sepolia PoW Faucet
1. Go to: https://sepolia-faucet.pk910.de/
2. Enter your wallet address
3. Start mining (browser-based)
4. Wait for ETH to accumulate

### Verify You Have ETH
1. Open MetaMask
2. Ensure you're on "Sepolia test network"
3. Check balance shows > 0.05 ETH
